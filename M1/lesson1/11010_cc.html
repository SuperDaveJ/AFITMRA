A Manufacturing Readiness Assessment, also commonly known as simply a MRA, focuses on defining the current level of manufacturing maturity and identifies the maturity shortfalls and associated risks of a technology, a component, a manufacturing process, or a weapon system or subsystem. This lesson presents the foundational knowledge for conducting a Manufacturing Readiness Assessment. In this lesson, you will learn about the definition, purpose, benefits, and drawbacks of a Manufacturing Readiness Assessment, as well as the steps in the Manufacturing Readiness Assessment process. You will also learn the results of reports regarding Manufacturing Readiness Assessments.
