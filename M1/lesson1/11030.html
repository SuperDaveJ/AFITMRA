Manufacturing Readiness Levels

MRL 1

In MRL 1, manufacturing feasibility is assessed.

MRL 2

In MRL 2, manufacturing concepts are defined.

MRL 3

In MRL 3, manufacturing concepts are developed.

MRL 4

In MRL 4, the capability to produce the technology is shown in a laboratory environment.

MRL 5

In MRL 5, the capability to produce prototype components is shown in a production relevant environment.

MRL 6

In MRL 6, the capability to produce a prototype system or subsystem is shown in a production relevant environment.

MRL 7

In MRL 7, the capability to produce systems, subsystems, or components is shown in a production representative environment.

MRL 8

In MRL 8, pilot line capability is demonstrated and we are ready to begin Low Rate Initial Production (LRIP).

MRL 9

In MRL 9, Low Rate Production is demonstrated and the capability is in place to begin Full Rate Production (FRP).

MRL 10

In MRL 10, FRP is demonstrated and lean production practices are in place.
