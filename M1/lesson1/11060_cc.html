Ms. Sherrill Buchanan

We need to take a minute here to delineate between the purposes for assessing manufacturing readiness in the Science and Technology environment and in the Acquisition environment.

The purpose for assessing manufacturing readiness in the Science and Technology environment is to successfully transition to a weapon system application. The purpose for assessing manufacturing readiness in the Acquisition environment is to ensure the industrial base to provide the vital components and materials for the weapon system application.
