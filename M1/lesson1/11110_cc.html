The Milestone A decision point marks the entry into the Technology Development Phase of acquisition. By the end of this phase, manufacturing capabilities should be brought to at least Manufacturing Readiness Level 6.

Previously completed Manufacturing Readiness Assessments will be used as baseline references. If no assessment was previously performed, one should be completed early in the phase. This review will be performed by a manufacturing team selected by the program manager and may possibly include manufacturing subject matter experts and other objective reviewers as required.
