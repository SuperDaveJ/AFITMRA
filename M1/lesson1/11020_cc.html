Let's get started by first talking about what we mean when we talk about a Manufacturing Readiness Assessment. A Manufacturing Readiness Assessment is defined as a formal structured evaluation of a technology, component, manufacturing process, weapon system, or subsystem. It uses Manufacturing Readiness Levels as a standard to measure that level of readiness.
