DoDI 5000.02 does not mention manufacturing considerations for the Material Solution Analysis Phase, but it is implied, as failure to address manufacturing risk in the development of the Technology Development Strategy can be a costly mistake, which can be very difficult to recover from in later program phases.

During the early Material Solution Analysis Phase, the manufacturing maturity will be determined using Manufacturing Readiness Level 3 criteria. This will be addressed through an initial Manufacturing Readiness Assessment within one year of the start of the phase.

There will be a final Manufacturing Readiness Assessment performed prior to Milestone A. This assessment will use Manufacturing Readiness Level 4 criteria to determine if all manufacturing risks have been assessed at the appropriate level and that an appropriate Manufacturing Maturity Plan is in place for the transition to the Technology Development Phase.
