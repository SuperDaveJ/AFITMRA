In addition to increasing the understanding of manufacturing maturity and risk, there are at least four other reasons to conduct a Manufacturing Readiness Assessment. The Manufacturing Readiness Assessment helps manage cost, schedule, and performance risk; it provides confidence to the weapons system development; it helps rebuild a shrinking support structure; and Department of Defense Acquisition policies tie Manufacturing Readiness Levels to milestone reviews.
