As mentioned before, the program office is required to prepare a Manufacturing Maturation Plan that covers all manufacturing risk areas. The Manufacturing Maturation Plan is created with the assistance of the Manufacturing Readiness Assessment report and the two are delivered simultaneously.

Below is an outline for a Manufacturing Maturation Plan. It includes the most essential items in planning for manufacturing maturity.

Mr. Arnold Jackson

Using the Manufacturing Readiness Assessment Report, it is my responsibility to prepare a Manufacturing Maturation Plan. In addition to the general content of the plan, it should address details such as what prototypes or test articles will be built, what tests will be run, how does the test environment relate to the operational environment, what threshold performance must be met, what manufacturing readiness level will be achieved and when will it be achieved.
