Each of the manufacturing risk areas addressed in the Manufacturing Maturation Plan will subsequently have a Mitigation Plan developed for it. This Mitigation Plan stays in effect throughout the lifecycle of the program.

Mitigation Plans should include four items:

  • Identification of the system, subsystem, or component.
  • A description of the manufacturing problem.
  • An assessment of the program impacts on cost, schedule, and technical risks.
  • Development of alternative solutions.
