Supplier MRA Plan

For those elements that will not be assessed on-site, a plan must be made to complete an assessment in another way. This plan is known as a Supplier MRA Plan.

Supplier MRA Plan

The Supplier MRA Plan should resemble the main MRA Plan. However, it will require a contractor agreement, as well as a determination of whether there are additional contractor and supplier contract requirements, including details about the scope and method of the MRA and a definition of the output.

Due to privity of contract, it is the prime contractor’s responsibility to take the lead in the Supplier MRA Plan.

From the implementation of a Supplier MRA Plan, mitigation effectiveness can be measured and updates to the assessment can be made.
