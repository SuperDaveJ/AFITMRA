Obviously, the goal of the Manufacturing Readiness Assessment is to achieve manufacturing readiness. Effective mitigation planning depends on the knowledge gained from all aspects of the program. Always keep in mind that knowledge reduces risk.

Mitigation Planning includes identifying where risks exist, defining those risks, and planning so that any potential problems caused by those risks can be avoided. Without this process, not only will risks continue to impede the program, they will grow and cause more problems as they proliferate.
