Readiness Levels are used within the risk mitigation process to evaluate and check on progress. Manufacturing Readiness Levels are the foundation of risk mitigation. The purpose of the Manufacturing Readiness Level tool is to identify manufacturing and production risk.

The identification of risk allows the Project Manager to create a plan or options to remove or reduce the risk. It also allows others to see a program's manufacturing risk and to see what is being done about it. Identifying risk allows for risk mitigation efforts, thereby making a program stronger and able to move forward. If risks are ignored you have a high potential for failure.
