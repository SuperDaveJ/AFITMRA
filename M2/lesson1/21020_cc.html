Ms. Sherrill Buchanan

A Manufacturing Readiness Assessment may be initiated at any stage of the acquisition program, most often by the Program Manager. A manufacturer or contractor may also conduct their own assessments, but it is ultimately the Program Manager’s responsibility to ensure the appropriate manufacturing readiness level has been reached by a given milestone in the program. The Program Manager will identify the program’s need for the assessment, which begins the process.

In addition to determining the need, the site for the evaluation must also be determined.
