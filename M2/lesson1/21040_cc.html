There are five triggers that determine a program’s need for a Manufacturing Readiness Assessment. They include an approaching milestone review; an Aeronautical Armament Center mandate; a request by the Program Executive Officer, Office of the Secretary of Defense, or similar party; good business sense; or as indicated by Department of Defense criteria.
