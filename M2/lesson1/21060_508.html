What are the steps associated with identifying the need for a MRA?

A Manufacturing Readiness Assessment may be initiated at any stage of the acquisition program, most often by the PM, who identifies the need for an assessment. In addition to identifying the need for a MRA, you will need to determine the site where the evaluation will take place.

What are the triggers for a PMO MRA?

  • An approaching milestone review
  • An AAC mandate
  • A PEO, OSD, or similar party request
  • A result of good business sense
  • A result of DoD criteria
