Technology Readiness Levels are not interchangeable with Manufacturing Readiness Levels. However, it is important to know how they fit together. For instance, a critical technology might be very mature, yet the manufacturing processes needed to produce it may be very immature.

Our understanding of the technology readiness levels gives us insight into potential problems and risks regarding technology maturity and design stability that we wouldn’t normally have. Additionally, by knowing the readiness of a system’s technology elements, we can determine what manufacturing processes are needed to produce it and whether or not they are in place.
