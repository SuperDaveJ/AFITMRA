There are five parts to performing the assessment. Some of the five parts are performed by the assessment team while the others are performed by the contractor. The five parts of performing the assessment are:

  • The initial meeting at the contractor site
  • Visits to the shop floor
  • One-on-one or small group discussions between the assessment team members and the contractor Subject Matter Experts
  • A private meeting with the MRA Team
  • An out briefing with the contractor
