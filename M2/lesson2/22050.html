What are the steps associated with planning a MRA?

The steps for planning a MRA include:

  • PMO or initiator of assessment identifies members of MRA Team and designates a Team Lead
  • DCMA is contacted to gather contractor performance information and act as liaison between MRA Team and contractor(s)
  • Team Lead
    • Evaluates and adjusts MRA scope
    • Defines MRA result deliverables
    • Conducts on-site pre-assessment team orientation
    • Schedules and conducts on-site assessment with contractor(s)
    • Delivers final report / briefing
  • MRA Team
    • Notifies the contractor(s) of the assessment
    • Provides contractor(s) with MRA information package
    • Provides contractor(s) with MRA schedule
