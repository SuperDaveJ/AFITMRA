A Manufacturing Readiness Assessment may be initiated by the Program Management Office, the Program Executive Office, the Office of the Secretary of Defense, or at the request of the manufacturer. This should be done several months prior to any key milestone decisions.

The initiator of the assessment must begin the planning phase. This involves identifying members of the Manufacturing Readiness Assessment Team and designating a Team Lead. When identifying the team, it is advisable to follow the best practice team requirements.

Select the Manufacturing Readiness Assessment Team requirements to view additional information.
